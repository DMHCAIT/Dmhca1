Musculoskeletal Ultrasound – Applications and Techniques

Date: 08/06/2024   Platform: Online on Zoom

About The Event

This webinar aims to provide orthopedic professionals with practical knowledge about the applications, techniques, and benefits of musculoskeletal ultrasound (MSK-US). It will cover the diagnostic and interventional uses of ultrasound for musculoskeletal conditions and train participants on the proper technique to enhance clinical decision-making.

What you'll learn

  • Gain a foundational understanding of how musculoskeletal ultrasound works and its applications in orthopedic practice.
  • Learn to identify common musculoskeletal conditions using ultrasound.
  • Understand how to use ultrasound for guided interventional procedures to improve patient outcomes.
  • Acquire practical knowledge of scanning techniques for various joints and soft tissues.

Event Content

  • Introduction to Musculoskeletal Ultrasound
  • Basic Ultrasound Physics and Technology
  • Applications of Musculoskeletal Ultrasound
  • Hands-on Techniques: A Step-by-Step Approach
  • Common Clinical Scenarios and Case Studies
  • Advances in Musculoskeletal Ultrasound
