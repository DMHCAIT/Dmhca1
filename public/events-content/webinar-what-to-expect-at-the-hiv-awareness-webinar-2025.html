Webinar: What to Expect at the HIV Awareness Webinar 2025

Date: 01/12/2025   Platform: Zoom Meeting

The HIV Awareness Webinar 2025 is set to bring together health experts, community advocates, educators, and individuals passionate about HIV prevention and empowerment. As global efforts to end HIV continue to evolve, this year's webinar focuses on delivering practical knowledge, breaking stigma, and highlighting the latest advancements in treatment and prevention.

Designed for both healthcare audiences and the general public, the HIV Awareness Webinar 2025 offers a valuable opportunity to learn, ask questions, and become part of the movement toward an HIV-free future. Attendees can look forward to an engaging, supportive, and informative event that empowers them with the knowledge needed to make a difference.

Our Speakers

  • Dr. Abhishek S. Lachyan - Topics: Bridging Gaps in HIV Care for Women: From Preconception to Postpartum
  • Dr. Azra Khan - Topics: Prevention and Stigma of HIV
  • Dr. Abbas Kazim - Topics: Digital Disruption: AI and Telehealth in the AIDS response
  • Dr. Dhanush Kumar Singh Asvin Bholoa - Topics: Adapting EWARS (Early Warning Alert and Response System)
  • Dr. Haricharan Modam - Topics: Transforming the AIDS Response
